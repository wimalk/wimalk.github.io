BRIEF NOTE NO 8 - by Wimal Kalapuge dated 31-7- 2020
FORECAST FOR PARLIMENTARY GENERAL ELECTION AUGUST 2020
This brief note is intended to record a forecast on the results of forthcoming parlimentary general election of Srilanka to be held on the 5th August 2020.
This analysis is done on the basis of several assumptions indicative of trends of behaviour patterns of voters in the previous elections.
The voter figures are approximately rounded upto the nearest thousand.
The procedure adapted has proved to be correct in the past and therefore deserved to be relied upon for the future as well.
Voter turnover may suffer severe deficit as a result  of adverse impediments now imposed as precautionary measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19
The overall result of this election indicates that out of a total of 225 seats 
PJP led by Mahinda Rajapakse will gain 132 seats ( which includes 16 national list seats)
SJB led by Sajith Premadasa gains 46 seats 
UNP led by Ranil Wickramasinghe gains 32 seats
TULF led by R Sambanthan gains 14 seats
EPDP led by Douglas Devanandan gains 1 seat
